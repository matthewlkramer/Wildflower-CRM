Keep the existing three-column cluster model and the new three-dimension coverage DTO. Refine the Donor/Purpose dimension into two explicit sub-states:

CRM linkage — whether the relevant payment units or bundle are linked to one or more CRM gift records, with valid amount coverage.
CRM record completeness — whether every linked CRM gift contains sufficient donor and purpose information.

A linked CRM gift is considered complete when any of the following is true:

It has a linked Donorbox record;
It has a linked coding-form record; or
It has a linked donor and every required allocation row has the necessary restriction fields completed.

Use one canonical predicate for CRM-record completeness. Do not reproduce the allocation-field rules separately in the cluster route, UI, and status logic.

Overall Donor/Purpose completion requires both CRM linkage and CRM-record completeness. Return both states separately so the UI can distinguish “no CRM record linked” from “CRM record linked but missing donor or restriction information.”

Derive Payment Transaction evidence independently. A Stripe charge is valid payment evidence whether or not it has a linked CRM gift. Donorbox is not payment-transaction evidence; it belongs only to the Donor/Purpose facet. Donorbox transactions ultimately move through Stripe, PayPal, direct QBO ACH/check, or another actual payment source.

QBO may satisfy payment-transaction evidence, accounting evidence, or both, based on the record’s economic role and relationships—not merely its imported QBO transaction type.

Overall cluster completion requires:

CRM linkage complete;
CRM record completeness complete;
Payment-transaction evidence complete;
Accounting evidence complete;
Amounts balanced under the canonical fee, refund, adjustment, and allocation rules;
No competing counted grains or unresolved conflicts.

All lens membership, counts, row status, available actions, and hydrated coverage must derive from this same canonical state. A row whose hydrated coverage is incomplete must never appear in Completed.
